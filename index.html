About Us

The foundation stone of BR Poultry Farms was laid on September 9th, 2016, and the farm officially commenced operations on November 17th, 2017. With over eight years of hands-on experience, BR Poultry Farms has become a benchmark for quality and consistency in the poultry industry. Our farm is dedicated to raising healthy layer birds for hatching eggs, ensuring strict biosecurity and optimal environmental conditions for bird growth.

We are proud partners with Sri Venkateswara Hatcheries since inception, and we maintain cutting-edge facilities that uphold the highest standards in poultry farming.

Our journey is led by Mr. Shaik Syed Basha, a seasoned engineer with over 25 years of international experience in the petroleum industry. His down-to-earth nature and unshakable dedication have made BR Poultry Farms a symbol of hard-earned success and integrity.

His wife, Dr. S. Razia, is a highly respected government school headmistress and social welfare contributor. She has received several accolades for her impactful work in education and society.

Their elder son, Mr. Babji, serves as the Managing Director, having completed his B.Tech from SRM University and an MBA. Their younger son, Dr. S. S. Rehaman, is a medical graduate from GITAM University and is currently pursuing MD in Pharmacology at Narayana Medical College.

Founder Profile

Mr. Shaik Syed Basha, the visionary founder and proprietor of BR Poultry Farms, is a seasoned engineer with over 25 years of experience in global petroleum companies. His exceptional journey is marked by hard work, humility, and a passion for excellence. Known for his simplicity and strong values, he laid the foundation for BR Poultry Farms with unwavering dedication and purpose.
